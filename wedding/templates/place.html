Parking

You can do drop offs at the hostel itself, but you cannot park there. There are many local long stay car parks within walking distance of the hostel. More specifically:
Hostel Parking
The hostel itself has limited parking for 10 cars, also 2/3 cars can park along the road leading up to the hostel. We'll probably reserve this for people with disabilities, core wedding organisers and folks staying at the hostel (in that order)
Dale End Car Park
Space for 50 cars, no maximum stay costing £1.40. This is the closest long stay car park, about 10 minutes walk from the hostel.
The Bridge - Ironbridge Road
Space for 108 cars, no maximum stay, costing £1.40. This is a little further away, roughly 15 minutes walk from the hostel.
