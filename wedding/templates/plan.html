So the plan is...

We're getting married! We'd like you to join us for a party to celebrate.

We will have the Coalbrookdale YHA from the evening of the 23rd May to the afternoon of the 26th May and will make sure someone is always on site to provide greetings and directions to the kettle! The basic idea is a party on the Saturday afternoon/evening with sitting around/exploring the local area for the other days.

The schedule

The Friday 23rd: folks arriving. Free evening, sort yourselves out for food etc.

On Saturday 24th we're having a formal shindig beginning at 3pm, ending late.

Saturday Party Breakdown
  • 3pm - greeting
  • 4pm - short ceremony
  • 6pm - buffet dinner
  • 8pm - band

The Sunday 25th is another free day, folks are welcome to hang around for another day. We have the hostel until the Monday at 2pm so no rush to leave.
