So the plan is...

We have booked a large Youth Hostel in Coalbrookdale from the evening of the 23rd May to the afternoon of the 26th May and will make sure someone is always on site to provide greetings and directions to the kettle! The basic idea is a party on the Saturday afternoon/evening with sitting around/exploring the local area for the other days.

Dinner Plans

Our plan is to construct a buffet cooked by our wedding guests. We've come up with a provisional "menu" (listed below), to cover the dishes we'd like to offer. If you'd like to cook one (or more!) of the dishes listed, please get in touch with one of us. We'll do our best to coordinate all of you, to try and minimise any doubling-up. If you'd like to cook something, but are a bit daunted by the dishes in the list, please still get in touch.
